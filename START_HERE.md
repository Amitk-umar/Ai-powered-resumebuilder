## 🎤 Presentation Tips

### Make It Engaging
- Start with a shocking statistic: "75% of resumes are rejected!"
- Tell a relatable story: "Imagine applying for your dream job..."
- Use analogies: "Backend is like a restaurant kitchen"
- Show enthusiasm: You're proud of your work, it shows!

### Make It Clear
- Speak slowly and clearly
- Use simple language (no jargon or explain it)
- Pause between sections for questions
- Point to diagrams as you explain
- Make eye contact with audience

### Make It Credible
- You built this - you're the expert!
- Answer questions thoughtfully
- Admit when you don't know: "Good question, I'll look into that"
- Show the code/architecture confidently
